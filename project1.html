Dolci Mango

Dolci Mango is a local frozen yogurt shop that brings delicious frozen treats to San Diego. I redesigned their site to make it easier to navigate, better reflect their branding, and create a more interactive user experience.

This included splitting the contents of the website onto separate pages, redesigning the page to make the contents more bold and minimal, making information more accessible (such as hours and location), and creating an interactive menu. The goal of the interactive menu is to create a fun, game-like experience, to appeal to younger customers. The addition of the Instagram feature page (the “Your Creations” page) is meant to increase customer engagement by encouraging them to feature the brand on personal social media pages.

Style Guide

Original Design

The design of the original website is very simple. However, it is a single page without page anchors, making it difficult to navigate. For instance, in order to find important information such as the contact, address, and store hours, one would have to scroll the entire page to find the information at the very bottom. There are also details, such as the cut-off menu and inconsistent capitalization, that could be improved upon.

At the beginning of my design process, my goals were to separate the pages to make the information more easily accessible, create a more "kid-friendly" website, and create a page that emphasizes customer engagement.
