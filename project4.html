Korean Performance Group

KPG is a K-Pop dance group at UC Berkeley. Alongside directing 10 dances, I have edited 13 professional dance covers using Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects. I also helped film several dance covers.

The covers I produced for KPG have reached 29.5k views and counting. I was always interested in video editing since middle school, starting on iMovie, then Inshot and CapCut (other free editing apps). After I had access to Adobe Creative Cloud Apps, the possibilities of editing became endless.

While I became interested in editing, I also became interested in learning about videography. I learned about camera settings, lighting, and how to use a gimbal.
