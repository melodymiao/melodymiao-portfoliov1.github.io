Checkt

Checkt is a to-do/calendar application for mobile devices. I designed this app to keep track of goals, to-do lists, and events.

I had the goal of designing an app where I could look at all my tasks, goals, and scheduled calendar events in one place. Many apps exist that are focused on one or the other.

Checkt integrates both into one app, making daily events and goals as well as future events and goals easy to input and view.

Style Guide

Competitive Analysis

Many “to-do” apps, such as the Reminders app on iPhone, make creating to-do lists extremely easy but do not provide a good overview of future events and timelines.

On the other hand, apps such as the Google Calendar app, make scheduling events very simple, but “to-do” tasks are small and require many steps to add/delete.
